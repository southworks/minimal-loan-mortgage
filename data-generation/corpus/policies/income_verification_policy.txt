Policy Ref: IN-330
Rule: declared income must align with verified payroll evidence and employment verification before it can be used for qualification.
Threshold: declared base income must align with verified paystubs within 10 percent, two recent paystubs are required for salaried borrowers, unverified bonuses or commissions are excluded from qualifying income.
Action: use verified income for qualification, deny when income cannot be verified and variance exceeds policy tolerance, manual_review when the variance is explainable but unresolved.
Exception: none.
