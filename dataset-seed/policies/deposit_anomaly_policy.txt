Policy Ref: BK-410
Rule: unexplained non-payroll deposits must be evaluated before funds can be relied on for qualification or closing.
Threshold: a large unexplained deposit is any non-payroll credit above 35 percent of monthly gross income, one unresolved large deposit triggers manual review, repeated unresolved large deposits may justify denial when funds are needed to close.
Action: use manual_review for isolated unexplained deposits, deny when repeated unexplained deposits undermine source-of-funds confidence in the MVP.
Exception: documented transfers between owned accounts are acceptable.
